About Us

"Puthiya India Puthiya Makkal Arakkattalai is a service-oriented voluntary and non-governmental organization established in 2022 and registered with the Anthiyur sub-registrar office in Erode under Reg. No: 14/2022 BK4, in accordance with the Indian Trust Act 1882.

Our mission is to address the challenges of ignorance, illiteracy, and exploitation that hinder the socially deprived and disadvantaged from improving their standard of life. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive support, including addressing physical, mental, educational, and social needs.

Together, we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of those in need. Join us in our efforts to create a brighter, more inclusive future for all."

Location of NGO

This NGO located in Anthiyur situated in Anthiyur Taluk, which is one of the 10 taluks in Erode district. Tamilnadu.

Project Areas

Each NGO when, started demarcated its projects areas to carry out its designated activities mention in the trust deed. Puthiya India Puthiya Makkal Arakkattalai had identified the whole of Anthiyur Taluk.

Qualifications of Trusteess

To become a member of the foundation, you have to donate Rs 100/- only to the foundation fund of the foundation along with the application form. The executive committee of the foundation will review the applications and select new members.

Both male and female above 18 years of age are eligible for membership in this foundation

Regularly attend the forum meetings held by the members of the Foundation. Failure to do so will inform the forum before the meeting

All the members should attend the meeting punctually. If the attendance register is marked and non-attendance for four consecutive meetings is given, the Trustees who do not attend the fifth meeting will be removed. The person who loses the membership of the foundation of the Foundation will also lose the donations paid by him and the donation paid by him will not be returned for any reason.

The Executive Committee will consider the applications received for membership of the General Committee and select the members based on their merit. No reason is required for applications rejected by the Board

Members of the Trust shall abide by the Rules and Membership shall pay an entry fee of Rs.100/- and an annual subscription of Rs.1200/- to become a member of the Trust.

Trustees should be honest. They should also have the attitude of giving up. It is not allowed to bring personal issues of political cinema as a topic of discussion in the members' council meetings

Members who contravene the rules and objectives of the Trust will be permanently removed from the Trust and such removal will result in forfeiture of gratuity.

Those in arrears of subscription cannot contest the elections. The membership fee must be paid by the end of March every year. Failure to do so will result in the removal of the membership from the membership at the next month's general meeting.

The trust fund should be used only for the development and service activities etc. of the trust

For Trust Members
Members are provided with an accident cover ranging from Rs 10 lakh to Rs 25 lakh
Children of members are provided free note book and uniform for education
All members are provided free medical check-up once in six months
Gifts will be given for weddings and auspicious events at members' homes
Free saplings are provided to all members once a year
The Foundation will help the elderly, differently abled and maids living in member homes to improve their lives.
24 hours free medical consultation for all members.
Free legal advice is provided to all members
